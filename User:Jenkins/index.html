About Me

  • Halfway through my 29th Solar transit
  • Aussie through and through.
  • ~20 years of Photoshop pixel pushing (about half that in Illustrator)
  • Between 15 and 20 years experience tinkering with computer hardware, rose to the title of Senior Computer Technician at a local PC repairs business a couple of years ago.
  • ~15 years audio engineering experience : http://soundcloud.com/jenkins
  • ~10 years writing scripts that should really be software
  • Another 10-ish years of web development and web designing, HTML, CSS, JS, some PHP and Ajax.
  • Run my own IRL business "Jenkins Media" and developing a second business "Novasphere"
  • Once upon a time, in a portable device land not far away, I was highly skilled at hard & software repair for the Playstation Portable (1000,2000,3000).
  • Have repaired countless other consoles, reballing, fixing RROD's and YLOD's
  • Own more HDDs than any human should - up to 86 now. (They multiply like rabbits!)
  • Also own more Tablet devices than a normal person should.
  • Go on shooting sprees often.... with a DSLR
  • Amateur astronomer and lover of all things cosmic

Game stuff

  • ~7 years of design annoyance via the Source Engine. Don't get me started on that.

  • Also Intermediate to advanced knowledge of Houdini3D and ZBrush
  • Advanced to Expert knowledge of e-on Software's Vue xStream.
  • About 6 years experience in FilterForge, lifetime licence holder :)
